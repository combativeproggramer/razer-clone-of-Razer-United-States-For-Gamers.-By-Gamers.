HOW TO REDEEM

  1. On Razer.com

    To redeem your Razer Gift Card on Razer.com
    1. Add your desired Razer products to your shopping cart.
    2. Proceed to checkout.
    3. Select the "Gift Card" option.
    4. Enter your gift card code.
    5. Click Redeem.

  2. In a RazerStore

    Present your physical or digital gift card to the cashier. The value will be applied to your purchase.

  3. On Razer Gold

    To redeem your Razer Gift Card on Razer Gold: 1. Visit gold.razer.comand log in to your Razer Gold account. If you don't have one, you'll need to create a free account. 2. Click your profile icon and select the "Reload" button. 3. Choose "Razer Gift Card" as your reload method 4. Enter your Razer Gift Card code. 5. Follow on-screen prompts to confirm the reload. You may need to complete a 2-step account authentication if prompted.

Beware of gift card scams. Do not share your code.
